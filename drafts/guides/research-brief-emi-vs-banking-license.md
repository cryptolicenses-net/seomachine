## Key conceptual points
- EMI = electronic money institution: issues e-money + provides PSD2 payment services. Cannot lend from deposits, no deposit guarantee, must safeguard client funds.
- PI (payment institution) for context: cannot issue/store e-money, lighter capital.
- Bank (full credit institution) = takes deposits + lends + deposit insurance + full prudential regime, EUR 5m capital.
- Switzerland: FINMA FinTech licence (Banking Act Art. 1b) — deposits up to CHF 100m, no interest, no lending; full banking licence separate.
