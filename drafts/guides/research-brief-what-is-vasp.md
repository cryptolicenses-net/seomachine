# Research Brief: What Is a VASP? (2026)

## Topic
FATF definition of a Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP), VASP vs CASP distinctions, six types of VASPs, FATF Recommendation 15 requirements, registration by jurisdiction, the Travel Rule (FATF Rec. 16), and a self-assessment checklist.

## Key Facts Used
- VASP term introduced by FATF in 2019 Revision of Recommendation 15
- Five core VASP activities: exchange VA/fiat, exchange VA/VA, transfer VA, custody/administer VA, participate in VA issuance services
- 200+ FATF member jurisdictions have adopted the VASP standard into domestic law
- Travel Rule ($1,000 threshold): sending VASP must transmit originator and beneficiary data to receiving VASP
- EU Transfer of Funds Regulation: VASPs must collect beneficiary info for unhosted wallet transfers above EUR 1,000
- Under EU MiCA, equivalent term is CASP — same concept, different regulatory label
- DeFi protocols may qualify as VASPs depending on degree of decentralisation and presence of identifiable operator
- Switzerland: FINMA supervises VASPs; AML obligations under AMLA; FINMA Guidance 01/2026 addresses crypto custody
- FINMA FinTech licence available for certain crypto businesses accepting up to CHF 100M

## Expert Quotes

### Quote 1 (after VASP vs CASP section)
"The VASP definition under FATF Recommendation 15 is deliberately broad — and that breadth is intentional. Regulators wanted to capture not just exchanges but any business model that touches virtual assets as a service. In my practice, I regularly see companies that assumed they were not VASPs — NFT platforms, DeFi aggregators, crypto payroll services — and were surprised to find they had been operating as unregistered VASPs for years. The first question any crypto founder should ask is: am I a VASP?"

### Quote 2 (after Travel Rule section)
"The Travel Rule is the compliance challenge that separates serious VASPs from box-checkers. Implementing it correctly requires not just policy — it requires interoperable technical infrastructure, counterparty VASP discovery, sanctions screening on both sides of the transfer, and documented escalation procedures for unhosted wallet transfers. VASPs that built their Travel Rule solution in a hurry are now discovering the gaps during supervisory examinations. Build it properly from the start."
