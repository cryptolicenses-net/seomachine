## Verified facts (each traced to a real source)

### EU — MiCA
- MiCA (Regulation (EU) 2023/1114) became **fully applicable 30 December 2024** for CASP/Title V provisions. Source: ESMA MiCA page; Dechert; Skadden.
- Unified single CASP regime across all 27 member states; passporting once authorised. Source: ESMA.
- **Grandfathering / transitional clause:** entities operating under national law before 30 Dec 2024 may continue until **1 July 2026** or until authorised/refused, but **member states may shorten or opt out** (some chose 6 months). Source: Dechert; ESMA Dec 2025 transitional statement.
- Stablecoins covered under MiCA as ARTs (asset-referenced tokens) and EMTs (e-money tokens); rules applied from 30 June 2024. Source: ESMA.

### US — fragmented
- **No single federal crypto framework.** SEC (securities / investment contracts) vs CFTC (commodities) split; enforcement-led historically. Source: WEF; Oxford Business Law Blog (via Wikipedia GENIUS summary).
- **GENIUS Act** (Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for US Stablecoins Act, S.1582) — signed into law by President Trump **18 July 2025**. First major federal crypto statute. Senate passed 17 June 2025 (68-30); House passed 17 July 2025 (308-122). Source: White House fact sheet; Congress.gov; Wikipedia.
  - Requires 100% reserve backing (USD / short-term Treasuries), monthly public reserve disclosures, issuer must be able to freeze/seize/burn on lawful order, marketing restrictions. Excludes compliant payment stablecoins from "security" and "commodity" definitions. Source: Sidley; Mayer Brown; Greenberg Traurig.
- **Market-structure legislation (CLARITY Act):** passed House 2025; would give CFTC exclusive jurisdiction over digital commodity spot markets, SEC over investment-contract assets. **Awaits Senate action as of late 2025/2026** — NOT yet law. Source: The Block 2026 outlook.

### UK — phased FCA regime
- **FSMA 2023** brought crypto within FCA remit; created framework / designated-activities approach. Source: FCA; Lewis Silkin.
- **FCA Cryptoasset Roadmap** published **November 2024** — multi-phase plan. Source: Sidley; FCA.
- **The Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (Cryptoassets) Regulations 2026** made by Parliament **4 February 2026**. Source: Sidley.
- New regime expected to come into force **25 October 2027**. Application period **30 September 2026 to 28 February 2027**. Pre-application support service (PASS) opens **July 2026**. Source: The Block; Sidley; Lewis Silkin.
- **Financial promotions regime** for crypto already in force (since Oct 2023); FCA Feb 2026 guidance on s21 approvers during transition. Source: FCA.

## KEY METRICS (infographic 1)
- EU MiCA fully applicable: Dec 2024
- US first federal crypto law (GENIUS Act): Jul 2025
- UK full regime in force: Oct 2027 (planned)
- EU regulators: 1 unified regime (27 states)
- US: SEC + CFTC (2 regulators, fragmented)
- UK regulator: FCA (single)

## TIMELINE (infographic 2)

4. 2025 — US CLARITY Act passes House, awaits Senate
5. 2026 — EU transitional period ends (1 Jul); UK Cryptoassets Regs 2026 made; UK application window opens (Sep)
6. Oct 2027 — UK full regime in force
