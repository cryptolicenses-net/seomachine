### Switzerland / FINMA
- FinTech license: accepts deposits up to CHF 100M or crypto custody; no investment of deposits, no interest
- Entity: AG (Aktiengesellschaft), GmbH or KmAG; registered office + business in Switzerland
- AML: must join an SRO (self-regulatory organisation) under AMLA
- No deposit protection in insolvency — must disclose to clients
- Contact: fintech@finma.ch | +41 31 327 16 16
- Source: https://www.finma.ch/en/authorisation/fintech/fintech-bewilligung/
- FINMA Guidance 01/2026 on crypto custody risks published January 2026
- Source: https://www.finma.ch/en/news/2026/01/20260112-mm-am-01-26/

### EU / MiCA CASP (2026)
- VASP label replaced by CASP from end-2024; full MiCA in force across EU
- 3 license classes:
  - Class 1: €50,000 min capital — basic services (custody, transfer)
  - Class 2: €125,000 — exchanges, trading platforms
  - Class 3: €150,000 — custody with large AUM
- Passportable across all 27 EU member states
- Lithuania: grandfathering period ended Jan 1, 2026; timeline 3-4 months
- Estonia: grandfathering until June 30, 2026; requires local office + resident director; €100-350K capital
- Poland: pending full MiCA implementation; VASP registration still 4-8 weeks; €10-20K advisory
