# Research Brief: Crypto License Cost Guide

## Topic
Full cost breakdown for crypto licenses in 2026 — budget tier (€3,000-€20,000), mid-tier (€25,000-€80,000), premium ($150,000-$400,000); hidden costs; ROI analysis; ongoing compliance costs by jurisdiction.

## Key Facts Gathered

### Three-Tier Cost Structure (All-In Year 1, Excluding Capital)

**Budget Tier**
- Poland (KNF/MiCA): State fee €616; legal €4,000-€10,000; total €5,000-€12,000; timeline 4-8 weeks; min capital €50k-€150k
- Slovakia (NBS): State fee €3,000; legal €5,000-€12,000; total €8,000-€15,000; min capital €5,000 (lowest in EU)
- Estonia (FIU): State fee €3,300; legal €7,000-€15,000; total €10,000-€20,000; timeline 3-4 months; min capital €100,000
- Georgia (NBG): State fee ~$500; legal $4,000-$8,000; total $5,000-$10,000; timeline 4-8 weeks; no capital requirement
- Bulgaria (NRA): State fee ~€500; legal €7,000-€16,000; total €8,000-€18,000; min capital €50,000

**Mid-Tier**
- Lithuania (VASP/MiCA CASP): Advisory €20,000-€35,000; reg fee €5,000-€10,000; total €25,000-€45,000; min capital €125,000
- UAE (VARA MVP/Full): Significant fees; timeline 6-12 months; min capital $50,000+
- Bahrain, Gibraltar: €25,000-€80,000 range

**Premium**
- Singapore MAS (MPI Major): Advisory $100,000-$200,000; reg fee $5,000+; total $150,000-$400,000; min capital $250k-$5M+
- Hong Kong SFC (VATP): Highest capital requirement globally; HKD 5M+ (~$640,000); rigorous technology/security review
- UK FCA: Historically 80%+ rejection rate; intensive preparation required; $150,000-$300,000 advisory

### Six Core Cost Components
1. Legal & Advisory Fees: 60-70% of total for mid-tier and premium
2. Government/Registration Fee: Fixed, varies by jurisdiction
3. Company Formation: Local incorporation, notarisation, translations
4. Minimum Capital: Retained in company as working capital (NOT an expense but affects cash flow)
5. Office/Substance: Physical presence, registered office, local director
6. Ongoing Compliance: Audits, MLRO, reporting, renewals (30-50% of Year 1 costs annually)

### Hidden Costs (Routinely Excluded from Quotes)
- Bank Account Opening: $5,000-$15,000 in advisory costs; 4-12 weeks timeline
- Professional Indemnity & Cyber Insurance: $5,000-$25,000/year (required by MiCA, Gibraltar, Cyprus)
- Technology & Security Audits: $10,000-$50,000 (MAS, SFC, FCA require independent tech audits)
- Regulator Queries & Resubmissions: 20-30% contingency of advisory fee
- Translation & Notarisation: €2,000-€8,000

### ROI Analysis
- Budget Tier (EU): ROI in 6-9 months; €10,000 investment pays back at ~€1,500-€2,000/month net revenue
- Mid-Tier (Lithuania/UAE): ROI in 9-15 months; requires €4,000-€6,000/month at €500k-€2M/month transaction volumes
- Premium (Singapore/HK): ROI in 12-18 months; requires $15,000-$20,000/month net; realistic only for institutional or high-frequency businesses
- Rule of thumb: < €200k/year gross revenue = budget tier; €200k-€2M = mid-tier; > €2M with institutional clients = premium

### FINMA/Swiss Context
- FINMA FinTech license: CHF 100M deposit ceiling; lower cost than full banking license
- Switzerland/Zug: Crypto Valley premium jurisdiction; high credibility but costs exceed all EU budget options
- FINMA Guidance 01/2026: New custody rules add compliance costs for Swiss-based custodians and exchanges
