# Research Brief — VARA vs ADGM Crypto License (2026)

**Intent:** comparison
**Primary keyword:** vara vs adgm

## Core distinction (verified)

- **VARA** = Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority, the **emirate-level** virtual asset regulator for **Dubai mainland, excluding DIFC**. Created 2022 under Dubai Law No. 4 of 2022. Standalone dedicated VA regulator with its own rulebooks. Serves **both retail and institutional** clients (with discretionary qualified/institutional-only designations). Source: vara.ae, u.ae.
- **ADGM** = Abu Dhabi Global Market, a **financial free zone** established by UAE federal legislation (2013) operating under **English common law**. Its financial regulator is the **FSRA** (Financial Services Regulatory Authority). Virtual assets regulated as part of the broader financial-services framework (FSMR). More **institutional / global-operator** positioning. Source: adgm.com, kslaw.com.

## VARA — verified figures

7 licensed VA activities: Advisory, Broker-Dealer, Custody, Exchange, Lending & Borrowing, Management & Investment, Transfer & Settlement (+ VA Issuance Category-1). Source: vara.ae licensed activities, 10leaves.ae.

Capital (AED, paid-up / higher of base or % overheads):
- Advisory: 100,000
- Broker-Dealer: 400,000 (VARA custodian) / 600,000 (other)
- Custody: 600,000
- Exchange: 800,000 (VARA custodian) / 1,500,000 (other)
- Lending/Borrowing: 500,000
- Management: 280,000–500,000
- Transfer/Settlement: 500,000
- Plus expense-based capital ~1.2x monthly opex held liquid.
Source: 10leaves.ae VARA guide.

Process: IDQ → 50% application fee → ATI (approval-to-incorporate / approval-in-principle) → Part 2 detailed submission (within 12 months) → 50% balance → FSP (Full Financial Services Permit). Provisional permit after ATI. Timeline ~12 months. Source: 10leaves.ae, whitecase.com.

Fees (AED): application 40,000 (advisory/transfer) or 100,000 (BD/custody/exchange/lending/mgmt); annual supervision 80,000 / 200,000. Source: 10leaves.ae.

## ADGM / FSRA — verified figures

License categories: Broker-Dealer, Exchange (MTF — operating a multilateral trading facility), Custodian, Asset Manager / Managing Assets, plus a new DeFi Protocol category (2025). Source: themiddleeastinsider.com, kslaw.com.

Capital: base capital from **US$250,000** for most categories; **custodian base capital raised to AED 5 million in June 2025**; MTF/exchange operators use **expense-based capital ~6 months operating expenses**. Capital = higher of base / risk-based / expense-based. Source: kslaw.com (June 10, 2025 amendments), 10leaves.ae, web search.

Asset rules: only "Accepted Virtual Assets" (June 2025 shifted to notification/self-assessment); **express prohibition of privacy tokens and algorithmic stablecoins**. Source: kslaw.com, adgm.com.

Timeline: FSRA targets ~4–6 months from complete application; complex (exchange/DeFi) 8–12 months. Source: web search, 10leaves.ae (120–180 days).

Milestone: **Binance secured a license under the ADGM framework** (institutional positioning). Source: adgm.com announcement.

3. VARA path: IDQ → ATI → provisional → FSP (~12 mo)

5. Ongoing supervision & capital maintenance
