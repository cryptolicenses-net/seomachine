# Research Brief: Fastest Crypto Licenses

## Topic
Speed comparison of crypto VASP licensing jurisdictions — ranking by processing timeline, banking access, and credibility trade-offs.

## Key Facts
- Georgia NBG VASP: 2–3 weeks, no minimum capital, Georgian banking only, ~€5,000 total cost
- Poland KNF VASP: 2–4 weeks, EU jurisdiction, SEPA access, €8–15k total, PKO BP/mBank accept
- Slovakia Trade License: 4–6 weeks, zero capital requirement, EU entity, no direct passport
- Estonia FIU VASP: 4–8 weeks, best banking (LHV, Coop), full SEPA, EU credibility
- SVG/Vanuatu/Seychelles: 2–6 weeks but severe banking limitations, major banks exclude
- MiCA CASP authorization: 3–6 months, provides EU single market passport to 27 countries
