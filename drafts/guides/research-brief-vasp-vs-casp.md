# Research Brief — VASP vs CASP (slug: vasp-vs-casp)

## Keyword + Intent
- **Primary keyword:** "vasp vs casp"
- **Secondary:** vasp to casp transition, mica transition deadline, casp passporting, legacy vasp registration
- **Intent:** Comparison (informational, MOFU). User wants the practical difference between the FATF/national-AML VASP term and the MiCA-authorised CASP, and what the transition means.
- **Title/H1:** "VASP vs CASP: EU Crypto Registration vs MiCA Licensing (2026)"

## Core distinction (the answer)
- **VASP** = FATF term (Recommendation 15, updated 2019). National AML *registration* regime. Pre-MiCA EU registers (Estonia, Lithuania, Germany BaFin, France PSAN, etc.) used the term and required AML/CFT compliance only. No EU passport. Purely national authorisation.
- **CASP** = MiCA term (Crypto-Asset Service Provider). Full prudential *authorisation* under Regulation (EU) 2023/1114. Grants EU-wide passporting from a single home-state licence. Capital, governance, custody, market-abuse, white-paper obligations far beyond AML.
- Migration: MiCA Title V (CASP rules) applied **30 December 2024**. Existing national VASPs got an optional grandfathering window of **up to 18 months → 1 July 2026** (Art. 143(3)), member-state dependent.

## Competitor structure (SERP)
Typical comparison pages cover: definitions, regulator/legal basis table, scope of services, capital, passporting, transition timeline, member-state grandfathering variance, what legacy VASPs must do, FAQ. Gap: most do not tie threshold/passporting back to *jurisdiction selection* for licensing — our practice angle.
