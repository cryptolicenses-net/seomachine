## Singapore (MAS)

- **Regulator:** Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS).
- **Primary license for crypto:** Digital Payment Token (DPT) service license under the **Payment Services Act 2019 (PSA)**. A firm offering DPT services must hold either a **Standard Payment Institution (SPI)** or **Major Payment Institution (MPI)** license depending on transaction volume.
  - SPI base capital: **SGD 100,000**.
  - MPI base capital: **SGD 250,000**.
  - MPI security deposit: SGD 100,000 (volumes up to SGD 6m/month per service) or SGD 200,000 (above SGD 6m). [Source: corporateservices.com PSA guide; MAS PSA 2019 guide PDF]
- **DTSP regime (FSM Act 2022):** From **30 June 2025**, entities providing digital token services *solely to customers outside Singapore* must be licensed under the **Financial Services and Markets Act 2022 (FSM Act)** Part 9. DTSP base capital SGD 250,000. No transitional period; MAS said it would grant DTSP licenses only in "extremely limited circumstances." Operating unlicensed: penalties up to SGD 250,000 and/or 3 years' imprisonment. [Source: mas.gov.sg media release 2025; Allen & Gledhill; Reed Smith; Moody's]
- **Retail access:** Permitted but MAS imposes consumer-protection restrictions (e.g. bans on lending/staking for retail, no incentives, risk-awareness assessments). Custody: segregate customer assets, hold on trust with FIs in Singapore, ~90% in cold storage.
- **Reputation:** Restrictive, high bar, "quality over quantity" — relatively few DPT licenses granted.

## Hong Kong (SFC)

- **Regulator:** Securities and Futures Commission (SFC). (Stablecoin issuers separately under HKMA.)
- **Primary license:** Virtual Asset Trading Platform (**VATP**) license under the **VASP licensing regime**, mandatory from **1 June 2023**. Legal basis: **Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Ordinance (AMLO)** + Securities and Futures Ordinance (SFO). [Source: SFC; PwC VATP regime; O'Melveny; Norton Rose Fulbright]
- **Capital:** Paid-up share capital **not less than HK$5,000,000**; plus liquid capital equal to at least **12 months of operating expenses** (rolling, no virtual assets counted). [Source: VATP Guidelines; Sidley; Lexology]
- **Retail access:** **Permitted** since the 2023 regime, subject to investor protection (suitability, eligible large-cap tokens admissible only if in at least two acceptable indices from two providers, non-security tokens, knowledge tests).
- **Licensed platforms:** ~**10–11** licensed VATPs as of 2025. [Source: Hacken; Dentons]
- **2025 developments:** 3 November 2025 SFC circulars expanded products/services and allowed integration of order books with global affiliate VATPs for shared global liquidity. [Source: Davis Polk]
- **Reputation:** Pro-active "ASPIRe" roadmap, retail-friendly, building HK as regulated crypto hub.
