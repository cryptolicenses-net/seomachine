## Core thesis (must be stated clearly)
The phrase "MSB license" is a misnomer. Federal FinCEN MSB **registration** is NOT a license — it is a notification/compliance registration for AML/BSA purposes. It does NOT authorize money transmission. The actual operating **licenses** are STATE money transmitter licenses (MTLs), required in 49 states + D.C. (Montana being the exception). A money transmitter, including most crypto exchanges/exchangers, needs BOTH federal MSB registration AND state MTLs.

| Fact | Source |
|------|--------|
| MSB defined at 31 CFR 1010.100(ff); categories: dealer in foreign exchange, check casher, issuer/seller/redeemer of traveler's checks/money orders/stored value (prepaid access), money transmitter, US Postal Service, provider/seller of prepaid access | fincen.gov/money-services-business-definition; ecfr.gov 31 CFR 1010.100 |
| Activity threshold of >$1,000 per person per day applies to most MSB categories EXCEPT money transmitter (no threshold) | fincen.gov/money-services-business-definition |
| Money transmitter = anyone who engages as a business in transfer of funds, regardless of amount | fincen.gov MSB definition; 31 CFR 1010.100(ff)(5) |
| 2019 CVC guidance: an administrator or exchanger of convertible virtual currency is a money transmitter (an MSB) unless a limitation/exemption applies; a "user" is not | fincen.gov FinCEN CVC Guidance FIN-2019-G001 (May 9 2019); builds on 2013 guidance FIN-2013-G001 |
| Federal registration via FinCEN Form 107, filed electronically via BSA E-Filing | fincen.gov/money-services-business-msb-registration; fincen.gov/registration-form |
| Form 107 must be filed within 180 days after the MSB is established | fincen.gov MSB registration; 31 CFR 1022.380 |
| No federal registration fee | brico/innreg secondary; fincen process |
| Biennial renewal — registration renewed every 2 years (24 months), filed by Dec 31 of second calendar year preceding the renewal period | fincen.gov MSB registration |
| Records (copy of registration + supporting docs) retained in the US for 5 years | fincen.gov registration |
| Re-registration triggered (e.g. agents increased >50%); filed within 180 days of triggering event | fincen.gov registration |
| MSB registration is NOT a license; does NOT authorize money transmission; state MTLs do | brico MSB vs MTL; fincen fact sheet (federal registration ≠ state authorization) |
| 49 of 50 states + D.C. require state money transmitter licenses; Montana is the sole exception | brico MSB vs MTL; remitso/innreg state guides |
| State MTL applications typically 3–18 months; per-state fees, surety bonds, net worth requirements | innreg / ridgeway secondary |
| MSB must implement written AML program, file SARs/CTRs, recordkeeping | fincen 2019 CVC guidance; irs.gov MSB info center |
| IRS is the BSA examiner/enforcement for MSBs (delegated) | irs.gov MSB information center |
